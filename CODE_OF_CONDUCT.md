# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

- Be respectful. Disagree on the merits, not the person.
- Assume good intent. Clarify before accusing.
- No harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or deliberate disruption.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions that violate these standards.
Repeated violations lead to a ban from this repository.

For issues, email the maintainer listed in the repo's main README.
